About Us!

At Paws & Reflect, we believe every dog deserves a second chance (and maybe a second breakfast). We’re a team of passionate dog lovers who’ve made it our mission to connect wagging tails with happy homes. From scruffy scamps to regal retrievers, we rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome pups with love, care, and the occasional belly rub. Our goal? To make the world a better place, one wet nose and wagging tail at a time. Because life’s better with a dog—preferably one who thinks your lap is their throne.
